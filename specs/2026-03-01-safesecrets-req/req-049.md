## Evidence
- `src/aws_config.rs` `AwsConfig::build_client`: uses `aws_config::from_env().profile_name(p)` for named profiles and `AssumeRoleProvider` for role assumption via `aws_config::sts`.
- `src/cli.rs` `Cli`: `--aws-profile` (`env = "AWS_PROFILE"`) and `--aws-role-arn` (`env = "AWS_ROLE_ARN"`) are global args passed to `AwsConfig::from_cli`.
- `src/main.rs`: constructs `AwsConfig::from_cli(cli.aws_profile, cli.aws_role_arn)` and passes it to all `cmd_ssm_*` functions.
- Unit tests in `aws_config::tests` verify `from_cli`, `is_configured`, and field round-trips.
