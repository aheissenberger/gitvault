## Evidence
- `src/ssm.rs` `cmd_ssm_set`: first line calls `crate::barrier::check_prod_barrier(repo_root, env, true, false)?`.
- `src/ssm.rs` `cmd_ssm_push`: first line calls `crate::barrier::check_prod_barrier(repo_root, env, true, false)?`.
- `ssm pull` and `ssm diff` are read-only operations and do NOT require the barrier.
- `check_prod_barrier` (REQ-10) returns `Err(GitvaultError::ProdBarrierDenied)` if the barrier token is missing or expired, preventing the AWS write.
